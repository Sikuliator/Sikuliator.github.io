Sikuliator

Sikuliator is a system for automatic execution of GUI tests distributed across many machines, backed by SikuliX. It is developed by Malte Schledjewski as a part of his studies at the Hochschule Kaiserslautern in collaboration with the Fraunhofer Institute for Industrial Mathematics ITWM.
